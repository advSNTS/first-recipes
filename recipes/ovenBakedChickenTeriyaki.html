Oven Baked Chicken Teriyaki Recipe

Image from Peakpx

Description

This is a tutorial for cooking a simple Oven Baked Chicken Teriyaki. It is recommended to be served with rice. Juicy Chicken breasts served with teriyaki sauce are delicious. Perfectly sweet, savory, and just a tiny bit spicy. This recipe is minimal and easy for everyone to cook.

Ingredients

For 1

  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ¼ cup mirin
  • 4 teaspoons grated ginger
  • 3 cloves minced garlic
  • ¼ teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Whisk cornstarch and water together in a small bowl until dissolved; set aside.
  3. Combine soy sauce, sugar, mirin, ginger, garlic, and red pepper flakes together in a saucepan over medium heat until the mixture starts to simmer.
  4. Slowly whisk in cornstarch mixture. Bring to a boil; reduce heat to medium-low, and simmer until thickened, stirring often, about 10 minutes.
  5. Pat chicken dry with paper towels; arrange in a baking dish.
  6. Pour sauce over chicken breasts, coating all sides.
  7. Bake chicken breasts in the preheated oven until no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear, 25 to 30 minutes.
  8. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (75 degrees C).
  9. Enjoy!
