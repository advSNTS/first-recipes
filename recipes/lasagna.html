How to make a Lasagna

Image from Freepik.

Description

This is a brief lasagna tutorial for cooking a simple recipe. Lasagna is a classic italian dish that consists in layers of flat pasta called lasagna noodles. In this recipe, it's going to be cooked with it's usual sauce; tomato-based, cheese and meat fillings. It's possible that the world "lasagna" comes from the greek λάσανα (lasana) or λάσανον (lasanon), meaning 'trivet', 'stand for a pot' or 'chamber pot'.

Ingredients

For 1

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • ¾ pound lean ground beef
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6.5 ounce)cans canned tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 and ½ teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1 and ½ teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • ½ teaspoon fennel seeds
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • ¾ pound mozzarella cheese
  • ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese
